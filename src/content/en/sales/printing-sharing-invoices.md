## Printing Receipts

### Supported Printers

Kelola works with Bluetooth thermal printers:
- **58mm** (2-inch) paper
- **80mm** (3-inch) paper
- ESC/POS compatible

> **Note:** USB and network printers are not supported for mobile. Use web version for these.

### Printer Setup

1. **Pair Printer:**
   - Turn on printer Bluetooth
   - Pair in device settings
   - Ensure printer appears in paired devices

2. **Grant Permissions:**
   - Bluetooth access
   - Location (Android requirement)
   - Storage access

### Printing Steps

1. Open completed **Stock Out** transaction
2. Tap **View Invoice**
3. Select **Receipt** tab
4. Tap **Print**
5. Select your printer
6. Choose paper size (58mm or 80mm)
7. Receipt prints automatically

## Sharing Invoices

### Share as Image

Best for quick sharing via messaging apps.

**Steps:**
1. Open invoice view
2. Tap **Share**
3. Select app (WhatsApp, Telegram, Email)
4. Image sends automatically

**Format:** PNG image of receipt

### Share A4 PDF

Professional format for formal documentation.

**Steps:**
1. Open invoice view
2. Select **Invoice** tab (A4 format)
3. Wait for PDF generation
4. Tap **Share**
5. Choose sharing method

**PDF Includes:**
- Invoice number
- Business details
- Customer information
- Itemized breakdown
- Bank details for payment
- Notes section

## Customizing Invoices

### Receipt Settings

Customize how receipts appear:

1. Open any invoice
2. Tap **Invoice Settings**
3. Adjust options:
   - Show/hide customer address
   - Show/hide business logo
   - Show/hide Kelola footer
   - Logo position (top/bottom)
   - Custom footer text

### Custom Footer Ideas

Add personalized messages:
- "Thank you for your business!"
- "Follow us @YourBusiness"
- Store hours or contact info
- Return policy

**Character Limit:** 200 characters

### Logo Options

**Top Position:**
- Logo at receipt header
- Professional appearance

**Bottom Position:**
- Logo after transaction details
- Standard POS style

## Digital Sharing Methods

### WhatsApp Sharing

1. Open invoice
2. Tap Share
3. Select WhatsApp
4. Choose contact
5. Send

**Best for:**
- Quick customer communication
- Sending copies to yourself
- Sharing with accountants

### Email Sharing

1. Open A4 invoice
2. Tap Share
3. Select Email
4. Address email
5. Send PDF attachment

**Best for:**
- Formal records
- Customer invoices
- Accounting submissions

### Save to Device

1. Open invoice
2. Tap Share
3. Select "Save to Files"
4. Choose location
5. Save

**Best for:**
- Offline access
- Backup copies
- Later reference

## Troubleshooting

### Printer Not Found

**Check:**
- Printer is on and paired
- Bluetooth is enabled
- Permissions granted

**Solution:**
1. Refresh printer list
2. Re-pair in settings
3. Restart app

### Print Quality Poor

**Solutions:**
- Check paper quality
- Clean printer head
- Ensure correct paper size selected

### Share Not Working

**Check:**
- Internet connection (for A4 PDF)
- App permissions
- Storage space

## FAQ

**Q: Can I print from the web version?**
A: Yes, use browser print for web. Bluetooth printing is mobile-only.

**Q: Can I edit an invoice after creating?**
A: Transaction details cannot be edited, but you can customize receipt settings for future prints.

**Q: Can customers pay via invoice?**
A: Invoices show your bank details. Customers transfer manually.

**Q: What if my printer isn't supported?**
A: Most Bluetooth ESC/POS printers work. Contact support for specific models.

**Q: Can I print in color?**
A: Thermal receipts are black and white. A4 PDFs support color if your printer supports it.
