Kelola.co utilizes a modern and secure cloud-based data storage system. Every piece of data you enter - from inventory records, sales transactions, to customer and supplier information - is automatically stored on our protected servers, not just on your device.

## Key Benefits of Our Cloud System

- **Data Accessibility**: Access your business information from any device (Android, iOS, or Web)
- **Automatic Backups**: Regular automated backups protect your data
- **Device Independence**: Your data remains secure even if devices are lost or damaged
- **Real-time Sync**: Data synchronizes across all your devices instantly

## Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

- **Encryption**: Data is encrypted in transit using TLS/SSL protocols
- **Access Controls**: Authentication mechanisms ensure only authorized users can access your data
- **Regular Security Audits**: We conduct vulnerability assessments and security reviews
- **Employee Training**: Our team is trained on data protection and privacy best practices

## Your Privacy

As an independent platform, Kelola.co has no affiliations with banks, fintech companies, or financial institutions. We are fully committed to protecting your privacy:

- Your business information will never be sold to third parties
- We do not share your data except with trusted service providers who help us operate our services
- You can request data deletion at any time
- We comply with GDPR, CCPA, and other applicable privacy regulations

For complete details, please review our [Privacy Policy](https://kelola.co/privacy).
