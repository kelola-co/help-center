## For New Users

### 1. Get a Staff Invitation Code

To join a business, you need an invitation code from the business owner. The owner will:
- Send you an SMS with the invitation code
- Or provide the code directly to you

**Important:** The invitation code is unique and can only be used once.

### 2. Download and Install Kelola

1. Download the Kelola app:
   - **Android**: [Google Play Store](https://go.kelola.co/android)
   - **iOS**: [App Store](https://go.kelola.co/ios)
2. Install the app on your device
3. Open the app

### 3. Create Your Account

Kelola uses Google Sign-In for account creation:

1. Tap **"Continue with Google"**
2. Select your Google account
3. Allow Kelola to access basic account information
4. Your account is now created

**Note:** You must use a Google account to sign up. Email/password registration is not available.

### 4. Join the Business

After signing in:

1. You'll see the option **"Join Business"** on the welcome screen
2. Tap **"Join Business"**
3. Enter the invitation code provided by the business owner
   - The code is case-sensitive
   - Enter it exactly as provided
4. Tap **"Submit"** or **"Join"**

### 5. Start Working

Once you successfully join:

- You'll be taken to the business dashboard
- You can start using the app based on your permissions
- Your activities will be tracked under your account

**What you can do depends on the permissions set by the owner:**
- Record sales (Stock Out)
- Record purchases (Stock In)
- View products
- Manage customers
- And more (based on your access level)

## For Existing Users

If you already have a Kelola account and want to join an additional business:

### Method 1: From the Home Screen

1. Open Kelola app
2. Make sure you're logged in
3. Look for **"Join Business"** option on the home screen
4. Enter the invitation code
5. Tap **"Join"**

### Method 2: Switch Between Businesses

If you're already part of one business and want to switch:

1. Go to **More** (bottom navigation)
2. Tap **"Switch Business"**
3. You'll see a list of businesses you're part of
4. Select the business you want to work with

**Note:** You can be a member of multiple businesses with the same Kelola account.

## Troubleshooting

### "Invalid Code" Error

**Possible causes:**
- Code entered incorrectly (check for typos)
- Code has expired
- Code was already used
- Wrong case (codes are case-sensitive)

**Solutions:**
- Double-check the code with the business owner
- Ask the owner to send a new invitation
- Make sure you're entering all characters correctly

### "Code Already Used" Error

This means:
- The code has already been redeemed
- Ask the business owner to send a fresh invitation

### Can't Find "Join Business" Option

**Try:**
- Logging out and logging back in
- Closing and reopening the app
- Checking if you're already logged into a business

### Joined Wrong Business

If you accidentally joined the wrong business:
1. Contact that business owner to remove you
2. Ask the correct business owner for a new invitation
3. Join the correct business

## After Joining

### What to Expect

- You'll see the business name at the top of the app
- Your permissions are set by the business owner
- You can only perform actions you're authorized to do
- Your activity is recorded and visible to the owner

### Getting Help

If you have issues:
- Contact the business owner who invited you
- Check the Help Center at help.kelola.co
- Contact Kelola support through the app

## FAQ

**Q: Can I join multiple businesses?**
A: Yes! You can be a staff member in multiple businesses using the same Kelola account.

**Q: Do I need to pay to join a business?**
A: No, staff access is included in the business owner's subscription.

**Q: What if I forget which business I'm in?**
A: The business name is displayed at the top of the Home screen.

**Q: Can I leave a business?**
A: Yes, but you'll need to ask the business owner to remove you from their staff list.

**Q: What happens to my data if I leave?**
A: Your transaction history remains with the business for record-keeping purposes.

**Q: Can I use Kelola without joining a business?**
A: No, you need to either create your own business or join an existing one to use Kelola.
