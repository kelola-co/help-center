## Customer Features

The Customer Features in Kelola.co are designed to help business owners efficiently manage customer data, track sales transactions, and analyze purchase history.

## Key Benefits

### Easy Access
- Customer data and sales transactions are neatly stored and accessible anytime
- Quick search by name or phone number
- Complete customer list organized alphabetically

### Enhanced Customer Experience
- Detailed purchase history helps you deliver more targeted and personalized service
- Track customer preferences and buying patterns
- Build stronger customer relationships

## What You Can Do

### Add Customers
- Create customer profiles with name and phone number
- Add customers during sales transactions
- Import customer data via web interface

### Track Purchase History
- View all transactions per customer
- See total spending and transaction count
- Track payment status (paid, partial, unpaid)

### Manage Customer Data
- Edit customer information
- View customer details
- Search and filter customers
- Delete customers (if no transaction history)

### Generate Reports
- Customer reports show top buyers
- Track customer purchase trends
- Export customer data for marketing

## Accessing Customer Features

1. Go to **Home** screen
2. Tap **"Contacts"** (Kontak)
3. Select **"Customers"** (Pembeli) tab
4. View, add, or manage customers

## Use Cases

- **Retail**: Track regular customers and their preferences
- **Wholesale**: Manage B2B clients with credit tracking
- **Services**: Maintain client records and service history
- **E-commerce**: Organize online buyers by platform (WhatsApp, Tokopedia, Shopee, etc.)

## Privacy Note

Customer data is stored securely and only accessible to authorized staff members with appropriate permissions.
