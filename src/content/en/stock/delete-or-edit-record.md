## Edit or Delete Records

Kelola.co provides features to edit or delete transaction records when data entry errors occur. This helps maintain accurate inventory and financial records.

## Who Can Edit or Delete

**Owner accounts only** - Only the business owner can edit or delete transaction records. Staff members cannot modify historical transactions.

## How to Edit a Transaction

1. **Open the Kelola.co app**
2. Tap **"History"** from the bottom navigation
3. Find and tap the transaction you want to modify
4. Tap **"Edit"** (pencil icon)
5. Modify the necessary fields:
   - Product quantities
   - Prices
   - Customer/Supplier
   - Notes
6. Review the changes
7. Tap **"Save"** to confirm

**Important:** Editing a transaction automatically updates stock levels and all related reports.

## How to Delete a Transaction

1. **Open the Kelola.co app**
2. Tap **"History"** from the bottom navigation
3. Find the transaction you want to delete
4. Tap the **three dots menu** (⋮)
5. Select **"Delete"**
6. Confirm the deletion

**Warning:** Deleted transactions cannot be recovered. Stock levels will be automatically adjusted to reverse the transaction's effect.

## When to Edit vs Delete

**Edit when:**
- Minor corrections needed (wrong quantity, price, or notes)
- Customer/Supplier information needs updating
- Date needs adjustment

**Delete when:**
- The transaction was completely erroneous
- Duplicate entry
- Test transaction that shouldn't be in records

## Best Practices

✅ **Act quickly** - Edit/delete as soon as you notice the error
✅ **Add notes** - Document why the change was made
✅ **Review before saving** - Double-check all changes
✅ **Inform your team** - Let relevant staff know about significant corrections

## Limitations

- Only the Owner account can edit/delete transactions
- Some transactions may be locked after a certain period
- Bulk editing is not available - edit one transaction at a time
- Deleted transactions cannot be restored
