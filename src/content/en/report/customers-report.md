## Customer Reports

The customer reporting feature in Kelola.co provides insights and analysis of purchases made by your buyers and customers.

## What You'll Learn

By the end of this guide, you'll be able to:
- View customer purchase reports
- Analyze customer spending patterns
- Download customer data
- Identify top customers

## Accessing Customer Reports

1. **Open the Kelola.co app**
2. From **Home** screen, tap **"View Reports"** (Lihat Laporan)
3. Select **"Customer Report"** (Laporan Pembeli)
4. Set your desired time period
5. Kelola.co will display customer transaction data

**Note:** Customer Report access requires permission and may require a paid subscription.

## What's Included

The Customer Report shows:
- **Customer name** and contact information
- **Total transactions** - Number of purchases made
- **Total sales** - Total amount spent by each customer
- **Profit** - Profit generated from each customer (if accessible)
- **Last purchase** - Date of most recent transaction

## Using the Report

### View Customer Details
Tap on any customer to see:
- Complete purchase history
- Individual transaction details
- Payment status (paid, partial, unpaid)

### Sort and Filter
- Sort by total sales (highest to lowest)
- Sort by transaction count
- Filter by date range
- Filter by specific customers

### Download Reports
1. Set your desired filters and date range
2. Tap **"Download"** button
3. The customer report will download in Excel format
4. Use for analysis, marketing, or accounting

## Benefits

- **Identify top customers** - See who spends the most
- **Track customer loyalty** - Monitor repeat purchases
- **Analyze buying patterns** - Understand customer preferences
- **Targeted marketing** - Use data for promotions
- **Credit management** - Track unpaid balances

## FAQ

**Q: Who can access customer reports?**
A: Staff with "readCustomerReport" permission can view this report. Contact your business owner for access.

**Q: Can I export customer data?**
A: Yes, tap the Download button to export to Excel format.

**Q: How far back does the data go?**
A: Reports include all historical data since you started using Kelola, subject to your subscription plan's date range limits.

**Q: Can I see individual transactions per customer?**
A: Yes, tap on any customer in the report to see their detailed transaction history.
