## How to Upgrade

Upgrading your Kelola subscription unlocks premium features and increases your limits for products, staff, and catalogs. Here's how to upgrade:

## Subscription Plans

Kelola offers four subscription tiers:

| Plan | Products | Staff | Catalogs | History Filter |
|------|----------|-------|----------|----------------|
| **Free** | 100 | 1 | 1 | 30 days |
| **Basic** | 500 | 3 | 3 | 180 days |
| **Plus** | 2,000 | 5 | 5 | 360 days |
| **Advance** | Unlimited | Unlimited | Unlimited | Unlimited |

### Feature Comparison

**Free Plan:**
- Basic inventory management
- Stock in/out recording
- Customer and supplier management
- Basic reports
- 1 staff member (owner only)

**Basic Plan:**
- Everything in Free, plus:
- Up to 500 products
- Up to 3 staff members
- Up to 3 online catalogs
- 180-day history filter
- Reports access

**Plus Plan:**
- Everything in Basic, plus:
- Up to 2,000 products
- Up to 5 staff members
- Up to 5 online catalogs
- 360-day history filter
- Multi-price support
- All reports

**Advance Plan:**
- Everything in Plus, plus:
- Unlimited products
- Unlimited staff
- Unlimited catalogs
- Unlimited history filter
- Priority support

## How to Upgrade on Android

1. **Open the Kelola.co app** on your Android device
2. **Tap "More"** in the bottom navigation
3. **Tap "View Subscription Plans"** at the top
4. **Choose your preferred plan**: Basic, Plus, or Advance
5. **Select subscription period**: Monthly or Annual (annual offers savings)
6. **Tap "Subscribe Now"**
7. **Complete payment** using:
   - Credit Card
   - Debit Card
   - Google Pay
   - Google Play Gift Card
   - Other payment methods available in your region
8. **Confirmation**: Once payment is successful, your account is immediately upgraded

## Managing Your Subscription

### View Current Plan
1. Go to **More** → **View Subscription Plans**
2. Your current plan is displayed at the top
3. See your usage: products, staff, catalogs

### Cancel Subscription
- **Android**: Manage through Google Play Store app
- **iOS**: Manage through App Store → Subscriptions
- **Note**: Cancellation takes effect at the end of your billing period

### Change Plans
1. Go to **More** → **View Subscription Plans**
2. Select a different plan
3. Confirm the change
4. Payment will be adjusted for the new plan

## What Happens When You Upgrade

**Immediate Benefits:**
- Increased product limits
- Additional staff slots
- More catalog slots
- Access to premium reports
- Extended history filters

**Data Preservation:**
- All existing data remains
- No data loss during upgrade
- Immediate access to new features

**Downgrading:**
- If you downgrade, excess products/staff may need to be removed
- You'll be prompted to reduce usage before downgrade completes

## FAQ

**Q: Can I switch between plans?**
A: Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect immediately.

**Q: What happens to my data if I downgrade?**
A: Your data is preserved, but you may need to reduce products, staff, or catalogs to fit the new plan limits.

**Q: Is there a free trial?**
A: The Free plan is available indefinitely. Some features may have trial periods for premium plans.

**Q: Can I pay annually?**
A: Yes, annual subscriptions are available and typically offer cost savings compared to monthly billing.

**Q: Do you offer refunds?**
A: Refunds are handled according to Google Play Store or App Store policies. Contact support for assistance.

**Q: What payment methods are accepted?**
A: Payment methods vary by platform (Android/iOS) and region. Common options include credit/debit cards, Google Pay, Apple Pay, and gift cards.

**Q: Can I use Kelola on multiple devices with one subscription?**
A: Yes, your subscription is tied to your account, not your device. Log in on any device to access your subscription.

**Q: How do I know when my subscription renews?**
A: Check your subscription details in the app (More → View Subscription Plans) or in your device's app store.
