## ✍️ Content Quality Standards

### Every Article Must Have:
- [ ] Clear learning outcome in title
- [ ] 3-5 minute realistic read time
- [ ] Step-by-step numbered instructions
- [ ] At least 1 screenshot (annotated)
- [ ] Related article links
- [ ] FAQ section (min 2 questions)

### Writing Checklist:
- [ ] Use "you" and "your" (second person)
- [ ] Start steps with action verbs
- [ ] Keep sentences under 20 words
- [ ] No jargon without explanation
- [ ] Specific numbers (not "several", "some")

## 📞 Questions to Answer Before Writing

For each article, answer:
1. Who is this for? (persona)
2. What will they be able to do after reading?
3. What do they need to know first? (prerequisites)
4. What screenshots are needed?
5. What could go wrong? (troubleshooting)
