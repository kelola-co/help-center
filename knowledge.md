## About Kelola.co  

Kelola.co is an inventory-first business-management platform built for micro, small, and medium enterprises (MSMEs). It centralises stock, sales, profit tracking, and staff collaboration in a single cloud workspace.  

### Why Kelola?
| Benefit | Details |
|---------|---------|
| **Real-time Stock Control** | View stock in/out history, receive low-stock alerts. |
| **Automated Financials** | Profit & loss and cash-flow reports without manual spreadsheets. |
| **Role-based Staff Access** | Up to 100 staff on higher tiers with granular permissions. |
| **Omnichannel Flexibility** | Works on Android, iOS, and any modern browser. |
| **Industry-Agnostic** | Used by retail, F&B, electronics, agriculture, cosmetics, and more. |

---

## Data Security  

Kelola stores all data on encrypted cloud servers with automatic backups. No data is shared with banks or fintech partners.

## Core Features  

### Inventory & Stock Management  
Kelola distinguishes **Stock In**, **Stock Out**, and **Audit** flows.

*New/updated capabilities*  
- **SKU, expiry date, and description fields** are supported for every product.  
- **Decimal quantities** are **not yet supported**; create items in their smallest sellable unit (e.g., grams instead of kilograms).  
- Bulk Excel import/export, low-stock alerts, FIFO cost tracking.  

### Customer Management  
Store buyer profiles, view purchase history, and segment top customers for promotions.  

### Vendor Management  
Record supplier data, attach purchase orders, and link payables directly to financial reports.  

### Online Catalog  
Share a public link to your product list so customers can browse stock and pricing online but cannot make purchases.  

### Barcode Scanner  
USB scanners supported on the web app: open *Stock In/Out/Audit* → **Scan Barcode** → scan items.  

### Invoice Printing  
Bluetooth thermal printers (48 mm & 80 mm) supported. Go to History → Stock-Out record → **View Invoice** → **Print**.  

### Reports & Analytics  
| Report | Contents | Export |
|--------|----------|--------|
| **Financial** | Sales, profit, cash-flow graphs | Excel |
| **Stock** | Incoming, outgoing, current balance | Excel |
| **Customer** | Transaction count & value per customer | Excel |  

### Staff Management  
Invite staff, assign roles, and monitor activities in real time. Owners can edit or revoke access anytime. Admin can restrict staff from accessing certain features such as COGS and Profit.

### Business Settings  
Edit your business information including:
- **Business logo** – Upload and change your business logo
- **Business name** – Update your business name
- **Address** – Modify business address details
- **Business category** – Change your industry category
- **Currency** – Switch between supported currencies
- **Date format** – Customize how dates are displayed
- **Bank details** – Add or update banking information for invoices
- **Delete business** – Permanently remove your business (contact support)

### Profile Settings  
Manage your personal account settings:
- **Profile name** – Update your display name
- **Profile picture** – Change your profile photo
- **Email** – Email address cannot be changed (contact support if needed)

### Plan Comparison  

| Plan      | Users | Item Limit | History Window* | Excel Import/Export | Wholesale Pricing |
|-----------|-------|-----------|-----------------|---------------------|-------------------|
| **Free** | 1 | 100 | 1 days | – | – |
| **Basic** | 3 | 500 | 6 mo | ✓ | ✓ |

> **Pricing Notice** – Actual prices vary by country, taxes, and app-store rules. Always check the **Subscription** page inside the Kelola app for current pricing.

### Upgrade / Downgrade Guide  
App → **More** → **View Subscription Plans** → choose tier → **Subscribe Now** → pay. Downgrades via support; remaining time is prorated.

### Billing FAQ  
- **Auto-renewal** – Enabled by default; if payment fails, plan reverts to **Free**.  
- **Payment methods** – Credit/debit cards, Google Play, Apple Pay, gift cards, plus local options.  
- **Refunds** – Case-by-case through support.  
- **One subscription per business** – Each business requires its own plan.

## Frequently Asked Questions  

1. **Does Kelola handle decimal quantities?**  
   Not yet. Create products in their smallest sellable unit (e.g., grams instead of kg).  
2. **Do products have SKU, expiry date, and description?**  
   Yes, all three fields are available.  
3. **What is the Online Catalog?**  
   A shareable link that shows your live product list with price and stock but customers cannot make purchases.  
4. **Does Kelola support multiple currencies?**  
   Yes – you can choose any currency worldwide. The currency symbol is set per business during onboarding and can be changed later in Business Settings.
5. **Which languages does the app support?**  
   Bahasa Indonesia, English, Thai, Vietnamese, Filipino, Arabic, and Malay.  
6. **What happens to my history data when my plan's History Window limit is reached?**  
   Your historical data is not deleted from the system. It becomes hidden based on your plan's history window (1 day for Free, 6 months for Basic, 12 months for Plus, unlimited for Advanced). When you upgrade to a higher plan, you'll be able to access all your previously hidden historical data again.  
7. **Can I import products from Excel or add products in bulk?**  
   Yes, you can import products from Excel or add multiple products at once using the bulk import feature. This feature is available on the web version at <https://web.kelola.co>. Import products from Excel is available for all plans including Free.
8. **Can I use Kelola on multiple devices?**  
   Yes, you can use Kelola on multiple devices by signing in with the same account or add staff to your business so that all the data in sync.
9. **Can I delete or archive a business?**  
   No, you cannot delete or archive a business. You can however, create a new business and ignore the old one.
